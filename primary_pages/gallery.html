Want to submit?

If you want your drawings to be featured in the Quilt of Loneliness Gallery, you must agree that your drawings may be published in my student thesis, in reports, and/or presentations and colloquia. The final thesis will appear in my personal website and it might also be part of the graduating exhibition at OCAD U.

No information (name, email address, etc.) will be collected.

But for the purposes of the Gallery, the drawing(s) and a caption that's under 280 characters describing your drawing(s) are required.

With that in mind, save your drawing, and send it eldrin.banares@student.ocadu.ca
